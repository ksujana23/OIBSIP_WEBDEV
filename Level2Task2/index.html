Biography

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, popularly known as A.P.J Abdul Kalam, was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a major role in India's missile and nuclear weapon programs and became known as the "Missile Man of India."

Dr. Kalam inspired millions of students with his humility, wisdom, and dedication towards education and innovation. His life remains a symbol of hard work, discipline, and patriotism.

Achievements

  • 11th President of India (2002–2007)
  • Known as the "Missile Man of India"
  • Played a key role in Pokhran-II nuclear tests
  • Received Bharat Ratna in 1997
  • Inspired millions through speeches and books
